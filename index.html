My story

Hello! My name is Srividhya and I began learning bharatanatya, at the age of 6, under the guidance of Smt. Janani Narayanan, Artistic Director of Nrityakalya Dance Comance in the Bay Area, California. As a part of Nrityakalya Dance Company, I have been a part of their major dance productions, and participated in numerous dance competitions both with her group and solo. After completing my arangetram in 2019, I participated in numerous workshops under various gurus including Lakshman Sir. In 2023 I began learning under Acharya Choodamani Guru A. Lakshmanaswamy through online classes and under him, she completed her second solo debut in Chennai "Nrityamalika". She is currently continuing classes under Lakshman Sir and is also spearheading the dance team at NYU called NYU Laya, NYU's first Indian Classical dance
